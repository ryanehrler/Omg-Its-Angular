Who am I?

My name is Ryan Ehrler. I have a Bachelors degree in Computer Science from West Chester University and have been doing professional developement for 5 years. I have been a lead developer on multiple Angular projects (AngularJS and Angular). In my professional life I have worked in a full-stack capacity, which just means I have control of the database all the way to the front-end. In my free time I have developed several side projects, the most exciting one I currently have going is a project which uses Electron, Angular, and Google Cloud. It analyzes first-person-shooter gameplay and provies analysis of that gameplay to the content creator which can than more easily edit their footage.

Why create this site?

Over the years I have been helping developers in my immediate orbit learn Angular, so I figured ... why not expand that orbit? Also I have disagreements with the approach most of the literature out there employs when teaching angular. Not that I have all the answers and I'm infinitely smarter than everyone else (even though I am... kidding).

... Honestly I really just want a soap box I can stand on and shout into the internet with. and maybe do some more learning myself.

No but seriously, I hope you learn something here because I most definitely will. For the most part I follow Best Practices outlined on Angular.io.
